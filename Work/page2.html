Backstory:
You live on a small farm on the outskirts of Ravensgate. Your father left your mother three years ago and
never returned back home. You found your mother, two months after your father left, hanging inside of the barn. She could not
deal with the grief of losing your father, and not being able to put food on the table for you and your younger sister, so she
took her own life.

Since then you have run the farm and slowly but surely increasing it's profit, season by season. It is just you and your sister
now dealing with the harsh realities of the world, together.

One day a courier came to your door with a letter for you. The letter stated that there was going to be a grand ball held within
the castle and you and your sister were invited. You did not want to go to this ball, but your sister has always dreamt of going
to one of the Queen's balls. Your sister begs and pleads with you to go and you finally give in. You spend the all of the money
you were saving up, to get a new plow horse, to get your little sister a dress for the ball.

It is now the night of the ball, what is in store for you and (your sisters name)?
